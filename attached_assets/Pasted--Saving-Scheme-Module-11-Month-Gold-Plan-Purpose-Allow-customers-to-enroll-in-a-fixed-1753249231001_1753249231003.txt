## üí≥ Saving Scheme Module (11-Month Gold Plan)

### üìê Purpose
Allow customers to enroll in a fixed monthly savings scheme, paying a fixed amount each month for gold. The **gold rate on the date of payment** determines the grams they earn for that payment.

---

### üßæ Saving Scheme Master

| Field                | Type                  |
|---------------------|-----------------------|
| `scheme_name*`       | Text                  |
| `jewel_type_id*`     | Dropdown              |
| `total_months*`      | Integer (e.g., 11)    |
| `description`        | Textarea              |
| `terms_and_conditions` | Long Text (HTML/Printable) |

**API**
- `POST /savings/schemes`
- `GET /savings/schemes`

---

### üìÖ Monthly Payments

| Field             | Description                              |
|-------------------|------------------------------------------|
| `payment_date`    | Date of payment (auto-today)             |
| `amount`          | Must match enrolled monthly_amount       |
| `gold_rate`       | Fetched from `price_master` on this date |
| `gold_grams`      | Computed: `amount / gold_rate`           |

> ‚ùó **Rules**:
> - Max `total_months` payments
> - 1 payment per month (enforced)
> - Auto-calculate grams based on gold price on payment date
> - Payment date must fall within scheme duration

**API**
- `POST /savings/payments`
- `GET /savings/payments?enrollment_id=X`
